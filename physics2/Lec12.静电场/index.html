    Lec 1 静电场¶

    1 基本公式¶

    公式
    库仑定律 \(\mathbf{F}_{21} = \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0} \frac{q_1 q_2}{r_{12}^2} \mathbf{\hat{r}}_{12}\)
    电场强度 \(\mathbf{E} = \frac{\mathbf{F}}{q_0}=\frac{q}{4\pi\epsilon_0r^2} \mathbf{\hat{r}} = -\text{grad} U = -\left( \frac{\partial U}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial U}{\partial y}, \frac{\partial U}{\partial z} \right)\)
    场强叠加原理 \(\mathbf{E} = \sum\limits_{i=1}^{n} \frac{q_{i}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0} r_{i}^{2}} \mathbf{\hat{r}}_{i}\) 或 \(\mathbf{E} = \int \text{d}\mathbf{E} = \int \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0} \frac{\text{d}q}{r^{2}} \mathbf{\hat{r}}\)
    电偶极矩 \(\mathbf{p}=q\mathbf{l}\)，方向与 \(\mathbf{l}\) 的方向（由负电荷指向正电荷）相同
    电偶极子电场中
    任意一点 P 处的电势    		 \(U_{P} = \frac{q}{4\pi\varepsilon_{0}}(\frac{1}{r - \frac{r_{e}}{2}\cos\theta} - \frac{1}{r + \frac{r_{e}}{2}\cos\theta}) = \frac{qr_{e}\cos\theta}{4\pi\varepsilon_{0}r^{2}} = \frac{p_{e} \cos\theta}{4\pi\varepsilon_{0} r^{2}} = \frac{p_{e} x}{4\pi\varepsilon_{0} (x^{2} + y^{2})^{3/2}}\)
    注：\(r \gg l\)，可由电势梯度求场强
    高斯定理 \(\varPhi_{\text{e}} = \oiint_S \mathbf{E} \cdot d\mathbf{S} = \frac{1}{\varepsilon_{0}} \sum q_{i}\)
    静电场的环路定理 \(\oint \mathbf{E} \cdot d\mathbf{l} = 0\)
    电场力做功 \(A_{ab} = \int_{a}^{b} q_{0} \mathbf{E} \cdot d\mathbf{l} = -(W_{b} - W_{a}) = -\Delta W = q_{0}(U_{a} - U_{b})\)
    电势能 \(W_{p} = \int_{p}^{\infty} q_{0} \mathbf{E} \cdot d\mathbf{l}\)
    电势 \(U_{P} = \frac{W_{P}}{q_{0}} = \int_{P}^{P_{0}} \mathbf{E} \cdot d\mathbf{l} = \frac{q}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0} r}\)，其中 \(P_{0}\) 是电势为 0 的参考点，可以是无穷远处（\(\infty\)）
    电势叠加原理 \(U = \sum\limits_{i=1}^{n} \frac{q_{i}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0} r}\) 或 \(U = \int \text{d}U = \int \frac{\text{d}q}{4\pi\epsilon_0r}\)
    电场与电势梯度的关系 \(\mathbf{E} = -\nabla U = -\left( \frac{\partial U}{\partial x}\mathbf{i} + \frac{\partial U}{\partial y}\mathbf{j} + \frac{\partial U}{\partial z}\mathbf{k} \right) = -\frac{\partial U}{\partial l}\)

    【Tip】 1. 由于电势是标量，用标量积分计算电势比用矢量积分计算电场强度简便得多，所以我们往往先求出电势，然后利用求偏导数或梯度的方法求电场强度 2. 负电荷记得带负号 3. \(\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{x^{2}+a^{2}}}=\ln \left(x+\sqrt{x^{2}+a^{2}}\right)+C\)，\(\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{x^{2}-a^{2}}}=\ln \vert x+\sqrt{x^{2}-a^{2}}\vert +C\)

    类型 电场强度分布
    均匀带电球壳 \(E = \begin{cases} \frac{q}{4\pi\varepsilon_{0}r^2} & (r > R) \\ 0 & (r < R) \end{cases}\)
    均匀带电球体 \(E = \begin{cases} \frac{q}{4\pi\varepsilon_{0}r^2} & (r > R) \\ \frac{qr}{4\pi\varepsilon_{0}R^3} & (r < R) \end{cases}\)
    均匀带电的无限长圆柱体或圆柱面 \(E = \frac{\lambda}{2\pi\varepsilon_{0}r}\)
    无限大均匀带电平面 \(E = \frac{\sigma}{2\varepsilon_{0}}\)
    两块带等量异号电荷的平行平板 \(E = \begin{cases} \frac{\sigma}{\varepsilon_0} & (\text{两板之间}) \\0 & (\text{两板外侧}) \end{cases}\)
    类型 电势分布（均可简记为 \(U=k\frac{q}{r}\) ）
    均匀带电球壳 \(U(r) =\begin{cases}\frac{q}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r}, & r > R \\\frac{q}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 R}, & r \leq R\end{cases}\)
    均匀带电圆环轴线 \(U(x) = \frac{q}{4\pi\varepsilon_{0}\sqrt{x^{2} + R^{2}}}\)

    【例题】 半径为 \(R\) 的非导体半球壳均匀带电，总电荷量为 \(q\)。求球心 \(O\) 处的电场强度。 【要点：微元的取法】 【解答】 将半球壳分解为环带，则每个环带的电荷量为 \(dq = q\sin\theta d\theta\)，\(dE_z = \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0} \frac{q\sin\theta\cos\theta}{R^2} d\theta\) \(\therefore E_z = \frac{q}{4\pi\epsilon_0 R^2} \int_{0}^{\pi/2} \sin\theta\cos\theta d\theta = \frac{q}{4\pi\epsilon_0 R^2} \cdot \frac{1}{2} = \frac{q}{8\pi\epsilon_0 R^2}\)，则 \(\mathbf{E} = \frac{q}{8\pi\epsilon_0 R^2} \hat{z}\)
